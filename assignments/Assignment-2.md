**1. Question 1: If you had to visualize the Titanic dataset, which chart would best show survival by gender and class - and why?**
**Answer:** A **grouped bar chart** or a **stacked bar chart**. Grouping by Ticket Class and sub-grouping by Gender effectively highlights the disparity in survival between demographics at a single glance.

**Question 2: How can poor design choices distort the message of a visualization?**
**Answer:** Misleading scales exaggerate tiny differences. Excessive color distracts from actual data trends. This violates data ethics by manipulating the viewer's interpretation.
